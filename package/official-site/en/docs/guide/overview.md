# Overview 🚀

TrailSnap is an intelligent **AI photo album** app. It helps you record, organize, and review your travel experiences, turning photos into memories worth cherishing.

In the future, every family will have its own AI data center. Your photo album is one of the most important data sources: it keeps the moments of your life, and AI can turn them into searchable, structured, and shareable memories.

## Highlights

- **AI Assistant**: chat with your album and search by natural language.
- **Footprints**: connect places into a travel trail on the map.
- **On This Day**: AI recommends the most memorable photos from past years.

## ✨ Main Features

### 🖼️ Standard Album

- Upload, manage, and browse photos and videos
- Smooth timeline browsing experience
- Supports **Live Photos** (iPhone, vivo, Xiaomi, OPPO, etc.)

![](https://blog.siyuan.ink/static/img/7f3995c1fa22ded5a26506194a516da4.timeline.webp)

### 🤖 Smart Album

- OCR, face recognition, smart scene classification
- Smart search (text, faces, tags, and semantic content)
- Smart/conditional albums generated by time, location, people, categories, and content
- On This Day (AI-curated memories)

![](https://blog.siyuan.ink/static/img/943063875ecd4ff82543e0ae3a21a4a4.AI-narrative.webp)
![](https://blog.siyuan.ink/static/img/d20dabc7c273d8f8d763f6251f1ec03e.AI-analyize.webp)

### 🗺️ Footprint Album

- View photos on the map
- Track lit-up cities (province/city/district)
- Show visited 5A scenic spots (supports custom scenic spots/locations)

![](https://blog.siyuan.ink/static/img/38bd45b7c69fe79457e74109dbba8683.map.webp)

### 🎫 Ticket Management

- Automatically recognize ticket information (train/flight/scenic spot tickets, etc.)
- Add, manage, and browse tickets
- Export simulated paper-style train tickets (PNG, red/blue styles)

### 📊 [Annual Report](https://siyuan.ink/annual-report)

- Photo wall, photo calendar, visited cities, farthest trip, seasonal photos, best photos of the year, etc.
